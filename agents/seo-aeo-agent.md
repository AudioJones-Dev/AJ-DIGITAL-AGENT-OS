## Purpose

The SEO/AEO Agent is planned as the search and answer-engine optimization agent for research, content opportunity mapping, visibility diagnostics, structured answer strategy, and reporting.

## Initial Responsibilities

- Analyze search and answer-engine visibility opportunities.
- Generate optimization recommendations.
- Track entity, topic, and content coverage.
- Feed approved insights into brand and client reporting workflows.

## Approval Notes

Production publishing, client-specific changes, and automated external actions require explicit approval until a dedicated agent policy exists.
